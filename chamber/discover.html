San Diego Skyline

San Diego was founded in 1769. As of 2021 1.382 million people live there. The biggest indrusties there are Health Care, Educational Services, and Professional, Scientific & Technical Services. The weather is usually between 50 and 75 degrees throughout the whole year. Many people love to call San Diego home.

USS Midway

The USS Midway is one of the most important historic ships. Starting in 1946 the Midway set itself apart by becoming the first Carrier to operate in the sub-arctic. In 1991 1,800 Americans were rescued by the midway to escape the erupting Mt. Pinatubo in the Philippines. Since 2004 the Midway has been in San Diego as the Midway Museum.

Old Town

As the name implies, Old Town is the oldest part of San Diego. Since its founding in 1769 it has been considered one of the main birthplaces of California. It features dozens of historic shops and is a great place to visit with family or friends.

Hotel Del

The historic Hotel Del opened in 1888 in Coronado CA. It has been a major attraction since its founding. Many don't know that it was the first building lit by electricity.

Belmont Park

Belmont Park

Belmont Park is a popular tourist attraction that opened in 1925. It features some rollar coasters as well as shops restraunts, and is a very short walk to the beach.
