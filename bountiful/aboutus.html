About Us

Bountiful Fresh Fruits and Vegetables was founded in 2014 by cousins Shawn Burton and Burton Spencer. They grew up spending the spring break helping their grandparents in the family garden in nearby San Jose California and loved the great family memories they have associated with the fresh fruits they would grow. At the end of spring break their grandparents would have family and friends over to enjoy their delicious fruit, especially the strawberries. Shawn and Burton want to share that same joy with all who love quality fruits and vegetables.
