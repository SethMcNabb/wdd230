Target Audiences

The target audience is those individuals, business owners, and investors who are looking to learn more about the businesses and local economy of San Diego California.

Personas

- Persona 1 - Jim is a business analyst who just moved to San Diego and has been given some freedom in his job to be a bit more creative in looking at smaller local businesses in the attemp to bring them on as a client for his consulting. He is 35, has 3 kids, and wants to spend as little time working and as much time with his family as possible. Because of this he would appreciate if the Chamber of Commerce's website was simple, clear, and had the resources he needed to better do his job.

- Personal 2 - Patricha is a new small business owner who is looking to expand her local business from a sucessful food truck to a brick and mortar location. She wants to learn more about what the process is for her to be able to do this.

Scenarios

  • Are there any upcoming events where I can promote my small business?
  • What are the legal requirements to start a busines in this city?
  • Is there any covid related relief available to a my business
  • What is our local government doing to help local businesses?
  • What are some of the largest companies in my city?
